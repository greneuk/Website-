FAQ
Frequently Asked
Questions
What does Grene actually do?
Grene joins your client assessments, transcribes the conversation, and automatically structures the notes against CQC key lines of enquiry and Home Office sponsor duties.
How secure is my data?
Grene is GDPR compliant, NHS DSPT certified, and working towards ISO 27001. All data is stored in UK data centres with end-to-end encryption.
Do I need tech skills?
Not at all. Grene is designed for care managers and staff. Just start your assessment and Grene handles the rest. No setup, no training required.
When is the official launch?
We're launching our beta in Summer 2026. Join the waitlist to get early access and help shape the product.
How much will it cost?
Pricing will be announced closer to launch. Early waitlist members will receive a significant discount on all plans.
What types of care providers?
Grene is built for domiciliary care agencies, supported living providers, care homes, and any CQC-regulated service that conducts client assessments or reviews.
