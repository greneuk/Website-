The Challenge

In England, over 1.1 million people use learning disability services [Source: NHS Digital]. Yet, critical behaviour data is often trapped on paper: 66% of care providers say paperwork reduces time for client care [Source: UK Care Guide]. 15+ minutes can be lost per incident on manual form-filling and filing. This leads to delayed interventions, higher staff stress, and poorer outcomes.

Our Solution: The Grene Way

Grene is the colour of regulation and growth. Our tools are designed to help individuals find their calm and thrive. We start by transforming the cornerstone of behavioural support: the ABC chart.
