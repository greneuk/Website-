Our Story

Our journey began in a small studio in Shoreditch, where we were surrounded by a community of creators and problem-solvers. We noticed a major gap: while technology was advancing rapidly, many crucial sectors, particularly learning disability support, were still relying on outdated, paper-based systems.

We saw an opportunity to bridge this gap by designing tools that are not just functional, but genuinely empowering.
