The Innovation
Innovation Viability Scalability
The CQC SAF mapping layer.
Built on lived operational knowledge.
  • Core
    An AI engine using retrieval-augmented generation grounded in the CQC Single Assessment Framework. It automatically categorises care records, incidents, communications, and supervision notes against 34 quality statements across 5 regulatory domains.
  • USP
    The mapping logic uses RAG enriched with domain knowledge from years of operating inside CQC-regulated services. No competitor has built this from the inside out. Manager corrections feed back into the retrieval layer, compounding accuracy over time.
  • Timing
    CQC's Single Assessment Framework final guidance is expected summer 2026. Grene's mapping layer is designed to update per guidance cycle, creating a continuous differentiation advantage.
IP Strategy
The protectable core is the SAF mapping layer: the proprietary logic that translates unstructured care operations data into structured, regulator-mapped compliance evidence. The retrieval corpus of operationally validated mapping decisions — where managers have confirmed, corrected, or refined the AI's categorisation — is a durable competitive asset that cannot be assembled from care records alone.

Competitive Moat
Innovation Viability Scalability
Why this is hard to replicate.
Validated mapping decisions, not just data
The advantage is not data volume. It is the corpus of operationally validated mapping decisions — thousands of cases where managers have confirmed or corrected the AI's categorisation. An incumbent holds raw care data. It does not hold the regulatory interpretation layer.
Incumbents face the innovator's dilemma
Care management platforms are architecturally built around recording, not regulatory interpretation. Adding compliance mapping requires a fundamental change to their data model. Their consultant referral partnerships would be cannibalised by a product that replaces consultancy.
Evidence history creates lock-in
Once a provider's compliance evidence history lives in Grene, switching means losing years of audit trail. The longer a customer stays, the higher the switching cost. Churn is structurally low.
Regulatory update cycle as a service
The CQC SAF framework updates. Every update is a retention event for Grene customers and a barrier for new entrants who must rebuild their mapping from scratch. Grene updates the layer for all customers simultaneously.

The mechanism
